Hove Restaurant

We are proud to present our digital menu! This choice allows us to save paper and do our part for the environment. As a sign of our commitment, we have planted trees with Treedom, an organization that deals with reforestation and sustainable development. Each tree planted has a positive social and environmental impact: It absorbs CO2 from the atmosphere, helping to combat climate change. It promotes biodiversity and the health of ecosystems. It supports local communities living in rural areas. By choosing to eat with us, you are also contributing to a greener and more sustainable future!
